Turns your mouse into the Portal cursor when using the portal gun
Adds indicators around the mouse that correlate to your portals in the world 

[Config Options]
- [c/FFFF00:Use Portal Cursor]: Enables when to use the custom portal crosshair
- [c/FFFF00:Use Portal Indicator]: Enables when to use the portal indicators
- [c/FFFF00:Indicator Offset]: How far from the center of the cursor to draw the portal indicators
- [c/FFFF00:Cursor Color]: Changes what color to make the custom portal crosshair
- [c/FFFF00:Sync Cursor Color]: Enable this to make the custom portal crosshair the same color as your main cursor
- [c/FFFF00:Invert Indicator Colors]: Switches the colors of the portal indicators
- [c/FFFF00:Use Custom Indicator Colors]: Whether or not to use your custom indicator colors
- [c/FFFF00:Left Indicator Color]: The color to make the left portal indicator (does not change your actual portal colors)
- [c/FFFF00:Right Indicator Color]: The color to make the right portal indicator (does not change your actual portal colors)
