Turns your mouse into the Portal cursor when using the portal gun
Adds indicators around the mouse that correlate to your portals in the world 

[h1]Config Options[/h1]
[list]
[*]Use Portal Cursor: Enables when to use the custom portal crosshair
[*]Use Portal Indicator: Enables when to use the portal indicators
[*]Left Indicator Offset: How far from the center of the cursor to draw the left portal indicator
[*]Right Indicator Offset: How far from the center of the cursor to draw the right portal indicator
[*]Cursor Color: Changes what color to make the custom portal crosshair
[*]Sync Cursor Color: Enable this to make the custom portal crosshair the same color as your main cursor
[*]Invert Indicator Colors: Switches the colors of the portal indicators
[*]Use Custom Indicator Colors: Whether or not to use your custom indicator colors
[*]Left Indicator Color: The color to make the left portal indicator (does not change your actual portal colors)
[*]Right Indicator Color: The color to make the right portal indicator (does not change your actual portal colors)
[/list]
